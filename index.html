Amsterdam WW2 Audio Tour

An audio walking tour through Amsterdam, guiding you past memorials, monuments, and historic locations from the Second World War. Developed by Erik Hamoen.

Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

  • Location: The app uses your device's location to show your position on the map and indicate nearby points of interest. Your location is never transmitted to any server and is never stored.
  • Audio: All audio files are included in the app. No audio is recorded or transmitted.
  • No accounts: The app requires no login and no personal information to use.
  • No analytics: The app does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
  • No third-party services: The app does not connect to any external servers or third-party services.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact supportaudiotour@gmail.com.
